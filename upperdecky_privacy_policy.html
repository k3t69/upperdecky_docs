Summary

UpperDecky does not collect or store any personal data. The app does not require accounts, does not use analytics, and does not connect to any servers. Push notifications are optional and off by default. This policy explains these points and how to contact the developer.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

UpperDecky does not collect, transmit, or store personal data of any kind. This includes names, emails, demographic information, health data, device identifiers, or analytics. No data is linked to any individual.

2. Push Notifications

Push notifications are available only if you enable them. They provide motivational reminders. No personal data is collected or retained. Notifications are handled through Apple’s APNs system and may be turned off at any time via device settings.

3. Third Parties and SDKs

UpperDecky does not use third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or any SDKs.

4. Health and Apple Health

UpperDecky does not connect to Apple HealthKit or any other health-tracking service.

5. Data Storage and Retention

No user data is stored or transmitted. There is nothing to delete or export.

6. Children’s Privacy

The app is intended for adult users (18+) only. It is not directed to minors, and no data is knowingly collected from children.

7. Your Rights and Contact

Because no data is collected, there are no personal data rights to exercise. For questions or issues, contact hammercalc@gmail.com.

8. Security

No user data is collected, but general best practices were used to secure the app. Report any concerns to hammercalc@gmail.com.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will be available at this link.

10. Health Disclaimer

This app is a supportive tool and is not medical advice. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider before making health or nicotine-related decisions.
